ABOUT US

Collegiate Internationals is an initiative of 'Collegiate Congress'. Officiated by international students, we aimed to bring all international students together, form a community, and help them in all aspects of their college careers. Collegiate Internationals organizes events which allow the students to share their ideas & culture, create bonds and build a professional network.
