Christo Thomas

(Principal / Co-Founder)

I am a student from India. I graduated with a M.A. in Urban Affairs from CUNY Queens. I am a strong proponent of democracy and had the opportunity to be involved in the democratic processes of two leading democracies. Currently, I serve as the Founder and Chairman of a leading student advocacy organization in New York City, the Collegiate Congress. Collegiate Internationals, a branch of Collegiate Congress, is a unique concept that entails bringing the international student community in New York City together. We attempt to promote cultural harmony and unity between international and nation students.
